Our Dreamy Story

Founded in 2024 by twin sisters with a shared passion for creating magical moments, DreamyTea has quickly become Winter Garden's most aesthetic boba destination.

What started as a dream between two sisters has blossomed into a vibrant community hub where every drink is crafted with love and every visit feels like stepping into a pink-hued paradise.

We're not just serving boba — we're serving dreams in a cup. From our signature Pina Colada with mango boba to our secret menu surprises, every sip tells a story of passion, creativity, and sisterhood.
