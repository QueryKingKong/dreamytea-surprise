Where Art Meets Boba Magic

While DreamyTea was founded by twin sisters Cristina Dewrell and Andrea Perlas, the shop's vibrant soul comes from their niece, Isabella Betz. With years of experience working at boba shops across Orlando, Isabella brought invaluable expertise in what customers love.

She personally hand-drew every illustration on the menu, helped craft the perfect offerings, and manages the shop's social media presence. Her recent addition of chai to the menu has already become a customer favorite.
